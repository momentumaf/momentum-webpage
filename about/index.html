About Momentum AF

Momentum AF is a strategic marketing consulting firm located in Chicago. Our focus is on our clients and local communities first. Our team works hand-in-hand with your business to create real-world solutions that improve business performance and efficiency.

Mission Statement

Our Mission is to grow our business with the same honesty and integrity we use to spread optimism and to align with your values. Through marketing and networking, we aim to build a team invested in trust, security, integrity, quality and respect; always promising client satisfaction.

Vision Statement

Our Vision is to Illuminate our clients' futures through providing exquisite solutions and services by spreading our passions for modernization. Along with our extended services, we enlighten your moments through our extravagant event management, financial assistance, and technical support.

Goals

Our goal is to provide our staff, clients and partners with a meaningful experience that will shift their future in the direction of continued prosperity. We believe that small businesses and entrepreneurship are pillars that ensure economic success in local communities. With economic opulence, we create more opportunities for communities to flourish while simultaneously improving the quality of life for all local residents.
