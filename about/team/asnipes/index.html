Amataziah C. Snipes

Co-founder and Chief Operation Officer

I have always dreamed of being my own boss. Dedicating my time and efforts to many companies, I realized that I was working to survive. I wasn't actually living, loving, or laughing; I wasn't enjoying my life. I chose to become a part of something that would make an impact on society in the years to come. While helping an associate of mine, I was introduced to a group of talented individuals who had a similar realization. And just like that, Momentum AF was formed to make my dream a reality.

As the COO, I govern client relations, project management, event planning, volunteer work, and internal operations. Each of these play an integral part of our business. Here in operations, we specialize in ensuring 100% client satisfaction.

In my free time, you can be sure to find me writing in a journal, behind a mic at an open mic, or in the kitchen working up a new recipe! My favorite food varies. (I'm a cook!) If I had to pick only one. It would have to be baked mac and cheese! Over the years, my hobbies and shifted and changed, but these have remained the same.

My passion in life has always been education. I never wanted to become a teacher, but instead I desired to lead people through knowledge. I chose to become an educator outside of the classroom to continue my purpose as I lay way to my legacy.
